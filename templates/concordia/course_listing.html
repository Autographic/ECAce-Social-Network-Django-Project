Okay, so what?

Well, while we are proud of our screen-scraping feat (thanks, registrar.concordia.ca!) we do admit it is, in and of itself, slightly useless. Unless, of course, you consider that all of that information is now resident on our database!

Not thrilled to bits yet? Well, then, try this out. It is kind of complicated, we are going to find a more streamlined way to do this, but this IS just a beta so please bear with us for now.

Log onto MyConcordia, go to your class schedule, then scroll down to the bottom of the page where it says in little invisible micro-print, Click here for a different display. (no, the word "here" doesn’t work on our page, or else we’d shorten these instructions a whole lot). Anyhow, click on the stupid tiny link and get the alternative version of your schedule: the one that lists the professors as well.

Got that onscreen? Great. Right-click on that schedule frame, and save the page to your hard disk. I promise you, this is going to get cool really soon now.

Share Your Class Schedule! Find Your Classmates!

Attach the HTML you saved from MyConcordia, click the checkbox under the form, and click the save icon. Note: You can ALSO upload future terms, or past terms if you really want to. And, if your schedule still has Professor T.B.A. listed, you can upload a new schedule anytime to update your records!

Okay, so what’s the point of this whole exercise? It’s pretty straightforward. It can be kind of hard to get in touch with your classmates, which can be a real PITA when you need to get in touch with them for your term project or lab report. Moodle tries its best, but in the humble opinion of this developer it still sucks. However, if you and your classmates upload your schedules, you’ll be able to send them messages from the system; you’ll have a more accurate list of who’s who (since we keep track of not only your course sections but also the lab and tutorial sections, and our profile design includes a space for a photo BIGGER than a postage stamp).
